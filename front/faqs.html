Frequently Asked Questions

How can I upload a movie to the platform?

You can upload a movie by going to the "Upload Movie" section, fill in the required details like title, description, genre, and upload the movie file. Once everything is ready, click "Upload" and wait for it to process.

What file formats are supported for movie uploads?

We accept common file formats such as MP4, AVI, MKV, and MOV. Make sure your file does not exceed the maximum allowed size, which is 5 GB.

Do I need to register to watch movies?

No, you can watch movies without registering, but signing up gives you access to additional features like creating personalized playlists, leaving comments, and uploading your own movies.

How can I leave a review or comment on a movie?

To leave a review or comment, visit the movie page and scroll down to the comments section to write your opinion.

Can I download movies from the platform?

No, movies are not available for download. You can only watch them via streaming directly on the platform. Once everything is ready, click "Upload" and wait for it to process.

What should I do if my movie upload fails?

If you experience issues uploading your movie, check your internet connection and ensure the file meets the format and size requirements. If the problem persists, contact our technical support for assistance.

Can I report a movie that violates copyright?

Yes, if you find a movie that you believe violates copyright, please use the "Report" button on the movie's page to notify us. We will review the content and take appropriate action.

How are featured movies selected on the homepage?

Featured movies are selected based on several factors, including popularity, user reviews, and content quality. Our curation team constantly reviews content to choose the best options.

Can I upload movies in other languages?

Absolutely, we accept movies in any language. We recommend including English or other language subtitles so that more users can enjoy the content.

How can I contact technical support if I have an issue?

You can contact technical support by sending an email to costumers@movies.com or using the contact form on our page. Our team is available to help with any issues you may encounter.
